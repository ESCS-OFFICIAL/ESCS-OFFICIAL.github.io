English Student Community of SMANSA

As the name suggests, we are a group of high school students who are really interested in the English language. You don't have to be good at English, because together we develop these international language skills that are in each of us. We will hold routine activities on Wednesdays, namely Regular Meetings or often called RM. So, at RM we will have fun together.

Starting from learning together to exciting games. In addition, within a certain period you will meet directly with real native speakers. What's even cool is that you can interact directly with native speakers and take photos with them. Apart from being an extracurricular member, we are also an organization in High School. There are many other activities that we will hold.

So, what are you waitin' for? Come and join us! We will be waitin' for our NEW FAMILY
